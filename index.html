About Us

Every repair has a story — and this is mine. HowToFix101 began as a passion project. It started with one broken laptop, a curious mind, and a student determined to learn how things work.

Step 1 — The Beginning

It all started when my own laptop suddenly stopped turning on. I didn’t have the budget for a professional repair, so I turned to the internet for help. Hours of watching YouTube tutorials and reading guides led me to my first successful fix — a battery replacement that brought my laptop back to life.

Step 2 — The Learning Phase

That small success opened my eyes to the world of DIY repair. I started learning more about hardware, troubleshooting, and maintenance. Every time something broke, it became an opportunity to learn — and to realize that fixing things isn’t as hard as it seems.

Step 3 — The Idea

After helping a few friends with their laptops and phones, I realized that many people were in the same situation — they wanted to fix things but didn’t know where to start. That’s when HowToFix101 was born: a space dedicated to sharing what I learned and showing that anyone can repair their own tech.

Step 4 — The Mission

Today, HowToFix101 stands for something simple but powerful — fixing is learning. I believe anyone can start with basic tools and online resources to repair gadgets, save money, and build confidence. Our goal is to make technology less intimidating and more empowering for everyone.

So whether you’re a student, a hobbyist, or someone who just loves tinkering, HowToFix101 is here to help you get started. Let’s make fixing things simple, accessible, and rewarding — one project at a time.

My Services

I offer reliable, high-quality repair and maintenance services focused on helping you restore, optimize, and understand your technology — all at affordable rates.

Hardware Repair

I fix common hardware issues such as battery failures, overheating, screen problems, and non-booting laptops using safe and professional repair techniques.

Software Troubleshooting

From system errors and slow performance to viruses and corrupted files, I diagnose and repair software issues to restore your system’s stability and speed.

System Upgrades

Want faster performance? I provide SSD upgrades, RAM installation, and optimization services to extend the life of your device and improve efficiency.

Guided Tech Learning

I don’t just fix devices — i teach you how to do it yourself. Learn basic repair skills with clear guidance, perfect for beginners and tech enthusiasts.
